About Us

Welcome to FirstAidNow — a community-driven platform dedicated to spreading first-aid awareness and empowering individuals to act confidently in moments that matter.

We believe that knowledge saves lives. In emergencies, ordinary people often become the first responders, and the actions they take can make a life-changing difference.

Our Mission

To build a community where more people are informed, prepared, and confident in handling emergency situations. Whether it’s a road accident, a burn, choking, fainting, or cardiac arrest, our guides provide clear, practical steps you can follow immediately.

Why It Matters

Across Nigeria, emergency services may sometimes take time to arrive. In those critical minutes, your ability to take quick, informed action — or even help keep someone calm — can make a major difference.

What We Offer

  • Practical first-aid and CPR essentials
  • Step-by-step emergency action guides
  • Tips to prevent common accidents
  • Important emergency phone numbers for Nigeria
  • Clear explanations based on widely accepted first-aid practices
  • Simple, easy-to-understand information for everyone

Our Vision

A safer, more informed society where people are empowered to look out for one another.
